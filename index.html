About Me

Hello! I'm Charlotte, a trainee welder based in Staffordshire with a growing focus on MIG and TIG welding. I take pride in creating strong, clean, and reliable welds—whether it's for practical builds or finer fabrication work. I came to welding from a very different background, but what drives me is the same: a love for hands-on work, learning new skills, and doing the job right. I’m always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow in this trade.

“I believe good welding is about more than strength—it's about precision, patience, and pride in the result.”
